Gifts From My Side
(paise nhi h mere par)
1. Life-long apne sath rakhunga.

2. Life-long kisi dusri ladki par line nhi marunga.

3. Life-long khush rakhne ki puri koshish karunga.

4. Life-long bina matlab kabhi daatunga-maarunga nhi.

5. Life-long hamesha jab tujhe need hogi tujhe seene se lagake rakha karunga.

6. Life-long jab bhi tere liye sochunga, tab selfish hokar nhi sochunga. 
...and many more(shadi ke baad pata lagengi)
